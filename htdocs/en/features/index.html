Zero Knowledge.

Sealas is the only Zero Knowledge accounting software:
We can’t access your data, even if we wanted to. In addition to using an SSL secured connection we also encrypt the data itself before it even leaves your browser.

Quick & Easy Invoicing

With just a few clicks, you will be ready to send your invoice to your client

Create invoices using customisable templates that already have everything you need. Add your clients automatically from your contacts, see previews, and store your invoice. Download PDF invoices quickly and easily.

Powerful insights to help you with your business

One-Click reports to give you an overview of your finances
