# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not only important to us, it is our core principle.

## App Data
The following information is stored in a retrievable format:
-	Your business contact and address information, which is required for generating invoices.
-	Your e-mail address in case you decide to subscribe to our newsletter.

All app data is stored encrypted, which means we cannot discern in any way what you are using Sealas for.

## User Tracking
We don’t save any access logs on our servers. We track visitors on the website through a privately hosted version of Piwik, which can be disabled through an adblocker or through the Do Not Track setting in your browser.
We only save the first two segments of a visitor’s IP address.

## User Rights
We don't sell or rent Users' Data to any third parties for any purpose. The only uses of Data are those highlighted in this policy. Users are the only owner of their Data and can request modification or deletion at any time.
